Ordering

To buy products and surfboards from Mitch’s Surf Shop, simply purchase them directly from our website by using the “Add to Cart” button and then filling out the checkout form. We accept Visa and Mastercard Credit Cards, and PayPal. Our online store is designed to provide you with a seamless shopping experience, ensuring that you can easily find and purchase the gear you need.

Shipping

We strive to get your items to you as quickly as possible. Here's what you need to know about our shipping process:

General Shipping Information

We usually ship within 1 week of receiving your order. Products other than surfboards are sent via U.S. Postal Service Standard Mail to ensure reliable delivery.

Coastal California Shipping

If you are located in Coastal California, the shipping costs for surfboards are as follows:

  • -7' boards: $105
  • -Boards 7'1" and higher: $115

USA Shipping

For orders outside of Coastal California, Mitch’s Surf Shop only charges you for the surfboard and packing costs; you will pay the surfboard shipper directly. Most orders arrive within one week. Your board can be shipped in one of two ways:

  • -To a home (more expensive)
  • -To a warehouse close to you (less expensive)

Prices fluctuate slightly, so please contact us for a recent quote to your location.

Board Packing Costs

For destinations other than Coastal California, we take special care in packing your surfboard. We bubble wrap and pack your board in a large shipping box. Up to 4 boards can be packed in 1 box, or 3 boards if they have glassed-on fins. Each shipping box costs $75, and the packing cost per board is $25.

Returns

For Shipped Items

We can’t accept returns on any used items or clothes that have been washed. If you need a different size for unused clothing, mail it back within 30 days of purchase and we will send you the new size. (We'll do this once and not charge for the extra shipping!) Please provide us with your return address. We can’t accept returns on any surfboards sold. All of our boards are sent with insurance; please check your board when it arrives and file a claim with the shipper if there has been damage.

For In-Store Items

Return store-purchased items within 30 days for a full refund. We don’t accept returns on items that have been used or washed. Additionally, we don't allow returns on surfboards, fins, or skateboards.
