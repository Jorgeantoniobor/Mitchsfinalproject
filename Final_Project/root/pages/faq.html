Ordering

To buy products and surfboards from Mitch’s Surf Shop, purchase them directly from our website by using the “add to cart” button and then filling out the form. We accept Visa and Mastercard Credit Cards, not PayPal.

Shipping

We usually ship within 1 week of receipt of your order.
Products other than surfboards are sent via U.S. Postal Service Standard Mail.

  • Coastal California

    7' boards are $105, $115 for boards 7'1" and higher.

  • USA Shipping

    Mitch’s Surf Shop only charges you for the surfboard & packing costs; you will pay the surfboard shipper directly. Most orders arrive within one week. Your board can be shipped in one of two ways; to a home (more expensive) or to a warehouse close to you. (less expensive) Prices fluctuate slightly, please contact us for a recent quote to your location.

  • Board Packing Costs

    For destinations other than Coastal California, we bubble wrap and pack your board in a large shipping box. Up to 4 boards can be packed in 1 box, or 3 boards if they have glassed-on fins. Each shipping box costs $75 and the packing cost per board is $25.

Returns

For shipped items: We can’t accept returns on any used items or clothes that have been washed. If you'd like a different size on unused clothing, mail it back within 30 days of purchase and we will send you the new size. (We'll do this once and not charge for the extra shipping!) Provide us with your return address. We can’t accept returns on any surfboards sold. All of our boards are sent with insurance; please check your board when it arrives. File a claim with the shipper if there has been damage.

For in-store items: Return store purchased items within 30 days for a full refund. We don’t accept returns on items that have been used or washed. We don't allow returns on surfboards, fins or skateboards.
