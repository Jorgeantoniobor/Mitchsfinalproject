Welcome to Mitch's Surf Shop!

Offering California's most innovative hand-shaped surfboards since 1967. We are your water sports specialists with two stores in the San Diego area: La Jolla and Solana Beach. Mitch's crew is always glad to help!

Mitch's La Jolla

Mitch's Surf Shop of La Jolla is located near the world-famous Windansea surf break. We specialize in a full range of surfboards, wetsuits, spearfishing gear, rentals, surf/skate clothing, and stand-up paddleboarding supplies. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you with finding the perfect gear for your water adventures. Whether you're a seasoned surfer or a beginner, Mitch's La Jolla has everything you need to hit the waves with confidence.

Visit us to explore our wide selection of products, including:

  • -Hand-shaped surfboards tailored to your surfing style.
  • -High-quality wetsuits for all water temperatures.
  • -Spearfishing equipment for underwater hunters.
  • -Stand-up paddleboards and accessories.
  • -Trendy surf and skate clothing from top brands.

Mitch's Solana Beach

Mitch's Surf Shop of Solana Beach is conveniently located just south of the Swamis and Pipes surf breaks on PCH 101. We boast a huge selection of surfboards, blanks, and resin shaping supplies, wetsuits, board and suit rentals, skateboards, and surf/skate clothing brands. Our store is a haven for surf enthusiasts, offering everything from custom surfboard shaping supplies to the latest in surf fashion.

Come visit us and check out our extensive range of products, including:

  • -A vast collection of surfboards for all skill levels.
  • -Premium blanks and resin for shaping your own boards.
  • -Durable wetsuits for year-round surfing.
  • -Skateboards and skate accessories.
  • -Stylish surf and skate clothing for men, women, and kids.

At both locations, we pride ourselves on providing excellent customer service and expert advice to help you make the most of your time in the water. Whether you're looking to rent equipment for a day at the beach or invest in high-quality gear for your next big surf trip, Mitch's Surf Shop has got you covered.

For more information, visit us in person! See you soon!
